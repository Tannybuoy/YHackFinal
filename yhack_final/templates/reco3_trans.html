Our Recommendation for Question 3


Although public transportation is recommended instead of using individual vehicles, vehicles such as buses, trains and subways also emit carbon dioxide depending on fuel consumption and the distance they travel. However, the transportation that causes the most swing among them is air travel. Adopting transportation, such as bicycles or public transport, instead of your car is essential for carbon emissions. By not using your car when it is not necessary, you can reduce your carbon emissions by 20%. Turning to small motor vehicles that consume less energy while buying a car will also be quite appropriate in terms of carbon emissions.
